Welcome To Little Angels Academy Provide High Standard of Education I am excited and honoured to be Director of Little Angels’ Academy . Promotion of high expectations and a love of learning are engendered by an inquisitive, investigative, questioning curiosity that will be developed in students across all key stages and in all subjects in our school.
